# Socials 8 Final Project

## How to run
Step 1: Download this repository (Click the green `code` button and select download zip)

Step 2: Open `Create Your Frisk 0.66 LTS 4.exe`

Step 3: Navigate to the mod menu and open `Socials 8 Final Project`

## Controls
`↑` or `W` for up

`↓` or `S` for down

`←` or `A` for left

`→` or `D` for right

`Z` or `enter` for confirm

`shift` for cancel and skip text

## SOURCES AND CREDITS ARE AT THE END OF THE FIGHT
